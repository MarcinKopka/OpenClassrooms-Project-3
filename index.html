Brooklyn Outdoor Film Festival 2018

Information about Film Festival

Organizer
Brooklyn Outdoor Film Festival Company
Place
Brooklyn Bridge Park
Time
5 - 8 August 2018 from 6pm to midnight

We invite you to the festival of films. Brooklyn Outdoor Film Festival Company just got the approval to show movies at Brooklyn Bridge Park this year from August 5th through 8th, from 6pm to midnight. We can't wait to help you discover awesome films! Shows are free and open to everyone. You cant miss it ! Please click the button below to register - registration will allow us to make sure that the place will be prepared for you !

Register

Films we are showing

We have 2 big screens! Every day, each of the screens will be dedicated to one of the great actors. This way you can watch movies with your favorite actor! Please take a look at the graphics below which reflects the days and films that we are going to show. In this way, we are sure that everyone will find something for themselves.
